Foxtail Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how This AI Life ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Foxtail mobile application ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign up or log in (e.g., via Google, Apple, or email), we may collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials.
  • User Content: Reminders, tasks, notes, and asset details you enter into the App.
  • Device Information: Information about your device, operating system, app version, and crash logs.
  • Usage Data: Interactions with features, settings, and in-app events used to improve performance and user experience.
  • Location Data (Optional): If you enable location features, we may collect approximate location information.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide, personalize, and improve the App’s features.
  • Send reminders, notifications, and suggestions.
  • Offer AI-powered recommendations (e.g., maintenance tips).
  • Monitor usage to improve reliability and performance.
  • Communicate updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

3. Sharing of Information

  • We do not sell or rent your personal information.
  • Service Providers: We share information with trusted vendors that help us operate the App (e.g., authentication, cloud hosting, crash analytics).
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.
  • Business Transfers: In a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred.
  • Aggregated/Anonymous Data: We may share non-identifiable statistics for research or business purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • We take reasonable measures to protect your information; however, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
  • Some data may be stored locally on your device for offline use.
  • Where applicable, industry-standard encryption is used for data in transit and at rest.

5. Your Choices & Controls

  • Access & Edit: You may view, edit, or delete your reminders and tasks at any time in the App.
  • Account Deletion: You may request deletion of your account and associated data through the App or by contacting us at support@thisailife.com.
  • Notifications: Manage push notification preferences in the App or your device settings.
  • Location Services: Enable or disable location features through your device settings.

6. Children’s Privacy

  • The App is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn we have collected information from a child without consent, we will delete it promptly.

7. International Users

8. Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted in the App with a revised Effective Date. Significant changes may also be communicated by in-app notice or email.

9. Contact Us
